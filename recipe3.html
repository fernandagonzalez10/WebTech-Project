Procedure:

  1. Wash and chop all of the salad ingredients with a sharp knife as small as you would like. Place in a large bowl.
  2. Add all of the liquid salad dressing ingredients to a blender first, and then add the rest of the dressing ingredients to the same blender.
  3. Blend until fully combined to a dressing consistency.
  4. Pour the dressing over salad ingredients.

A good dressing, according to Rebekah Peppler, makes a good salad outstanding and a great salad unforgettable. And the dressing we're making for this salad is just delicious. With earthy walnuts, crisp acid, and just a hint of zest, it elevates an already excellent salad to new heights. 

Cooking Time: 20 min

Difficulty: Extra Easy

Origin of Dish: USA

Ingredient List

  • 1 small head of Green Cabbage
  • 3-4 baby Cucumbers (or 1 large Cucumber)
  • 1/4 cup of Chives
  • 1 bundle of Green Onions or Scallions
  • 1 cup of Basil Leaves
  • 1 cup of Spinach Leaves
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/3 cup nutritional yeast
