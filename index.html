Fernanda's Cookbook is a one-woman show. This website's goal is to offer the recipes that amateurs like me enjoy cooking. My ultimate purpose is to share my love letter to food and the joy of making it, documenting it, and eating it. I hope you get the opportunity to explore the magic of kitchen arts-and-crafts and hopefully join this wonderful and welcoming community. Bon appetit!
