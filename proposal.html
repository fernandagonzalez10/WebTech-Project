Fernanda's Baking Cookbook

What is my website about?

My website is about baked goods. It is intended to serve as a compilation of recipes as well as other information about the preparation and cooking of my favorite baking recipes.

What is the objective of this website?

I have always been passionate about baking and this project serves as a treasury of the enjoyment of consumable masterpieces. The objective of this website is to share amazing recipes with those who share my same passion. I want to provide a guide on how to create simple yet delicious treats for the intended audience's recreational purposes. In addition to providing detailed instructions, tips, and tricks, I also intend to provide idea recipes that celebrate my personal view and traditions towards baking.

Who is the target audience?

The target audience of my project are amateur bakers who share my same passion for baking. These individuals are looking for easy-to-follow recipes that have ingredients that are easily accessible to all. This webpage can appeal to anyone who has the minimum interest in learning the basics of baking and wants to learn how to do it on their own.
